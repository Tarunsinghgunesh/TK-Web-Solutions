Website Launch Ke Baad Kya Kare — 10 Zaroori Steps

Tarun Singh — TK Web Solutions March 2026 9 min padhne mein Bharatpur, Rajasthan

Bahut log sochte hain ki website live hote hi kaam ho gaya — lekin yeh sach nahi hai. Website launch toh shuruat hai! Asli kaam launch ke baad hota hai.

Agar aapne recently website banwai hai — ya TK Web Solutions se banwa rahe hain — toh yeh 10 steps zaroor karo. Inse aapki website Google pe rank karegi, traffic aayega, aur real business leads milenge.

Step 1 — Google Search Console Mein Submit Karo

Google Search Console free tool hai jisse Google ko batate hain ki aapki website exist karti hai:

  • search.google.com/search-console pe jaao
  • Apni website add karo
  • Verify karo (HTML file ya DNS method)
  • Sitemap submit karo: yourwebsite.com/sitemap.xml

Iske baad 3-7 din mein Google aapki website crawl karna shuru kar deta hai.

Important: TK Web Solutions se website banwane pe hum yeh sab setup kar dete hain — Google Search Console, sitemap, aur indexing request sab included hai!

Step 2 — Google Business Profile Update Karo

Google Maps pe aapka business dikhna chahiye. Google Business Profile update karo:

  • Website link add karo
  • Photos add karo — business ki, team ki, kaam ki
  • Hours update karo
  • Services list karo
  • Existing customers se reviews maango — 5 star reviews bahut important hain!

Step 3 — Social Media Pe Website Share Karo

  • Instagram pe website launch announce karo — story + post + reel banao
  • Facebook pe share karo — local groups mein bhi
  • WhatsApp status pe link daalo
  • WhatsApp contacts ko personally message karo
  • Friends aur family se share karwao

Step 4 — Free Business Directories Mein List Karo

Yeh free listings karo — backlinks milte hain aur local SEO improve hoti hai:

  • JustDial — justdial.com/add-listing
  • Sulekha — sulekha.com/post-your-requirement
  • IndiaMART — indiamart.com
  • TradeIndia — tradeindia.com
  • Bing Places — bingplaces.com (Google ka competitor)

Step 5 — Website Speed Check Karo

Slow website = users chale jaate hain + Google rank nahi karta:

  • pagespeed.web.dev pe jaao
  • Apna URL enter karo
  • Mobile score 70+ hona chahiye
  • Images compress karo agar slow ho

Step 6 — Contact Form Test Karo

Website launch hone ke baad form test karo khud — submit karo aur dekho email aata hai ya nahi. Bahut baar forms kaam nahi karte aur leads miss ho jaati hain!

Step 7 — Mobile Pe Check Karo

Khud 3-4 alag phones pe website open karo:

  • Kya sab theek dikh raha hai?
  • Buttons kaam kar rahe hain?
  • Phone number click karne pe call lagg raha hai?
  • WhatsApp button kaam kar raha hai?

Step 8 — Regular Blog Posts Likhna Shuru Karo

Blog posts se Google pe rank karne mein bahut help milti hai:

  • Mahine mein 4 posts minimum
  • Local keywords use karo — "coaching bharatpur", "school website rajasthan"
  • 500-1000 words per post
  • Questions ke jawab likho jo log Google pe search karte hain

Step 9 — Google Analytics Setup Karo

Google Analytics se pata chalta hai:

  • Kitne log aa rahe hain website pe?
  • Kahan se aa rahe hain — Google, Instagram, Direct?
  • Kaunsa page sabse zyada dekhte hain?
  • Kitne der tak ruke?

analytics.google.com pe free account banao aur tracking code website mein add karo.

Step 10 — Monthly Review Karo

Har mahine check karo:

  • Search Console — kaunse keywords se log aa rahe hain
  • Analytics — traffic badh raha hai ya nahi
  • Google ranking — "coaching bharatpur" search pe kahan hain
  • Contact forms — kitne leads aaye
Yaad Rakho: Website ek baar banao, phir continuously improve karte rehte hain. Jitna zyada active rahoge — Google pe ranking utni acchi hogi aur naye customers utne zyada aayenge!
