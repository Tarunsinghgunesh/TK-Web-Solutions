WhatsApp Se Business Leads Kaise Generate Kare — Complete Guide 2026

Tarun Singh — TK Web Solutions March 2026 7 min padhne mein Bharatpur, Rajasthan

WhatsApp India mein 50 crore se zyada log use karte hain — yeh number 1 messaging platform hai. Aur sabse achhi baat? Yeh bilkul free hai aur aapke potential customers pehle se hi isko use kar rahe hain.

Bharatpur ke coaching centers, local businesses aur shops ke liye WhatsApp ek powerful lead generation tool hai — agar sahi se use kiya jaaye.

Step 1 — WhatsApp Business App Download Karo

Normal WhatsApp nahi — WhatsApp Business app use karo. Yeh free hai aur deta hai:

  • Business Profile — naam, address, website, category
  • Quick Replies — ek tap pe common answers
  • Auto Reply — raat ko bhi "hum kal reply karenge" message
  • Catalog — apni services/products showcase karo
  • Labels — leads organize karo (New Lead, Follow Up, Closed)

Step 2 — Business Profile Perfect Banao

  • Business Name: TK Web Solutions — Bharatpur
  • Category: Website & App Development
  • Description: Professional websites ₹5,000 se. Coaching, school, business. 5-7 din LIVE. Free demo!
  • Website: Apni website ka URL
  • Address: Bharatpur, Rajasthan
  • Hours: 9 AM – 8 PM, Mon-Sat
Pro Tip: WhatsApp Business profile mein website link daalna bahut zaroori hai — log directly aapki services aur pricing dekh sake.

Step 3 — WhatsApp Link Banao — Asaan Contact ke liye

Apna WhatsApp link banao jo click karne pe seedha chat khule:

  • Format: https://wa.me/919079368240
  • Pre-filled message ke saath: https://wa.me/919079368240?text=Hi!%20Website%20banwani%20hai
  • Yeh link website pe, Instagram bio pe, Facebook pe, everywhere daalo
  • QR code bhi banao — offline marketing ke liye

Step 4 — Broadcast List — Ek Saath 256 Log

WhatsApp Broadcast se ek baar mein 256 log tak message bhej sakte ho — aur unhe lagta hai personal message hai:

  • Category wise lists banao — Coaching, Schools, Businesses
  • Rozana 1 useful tip bhejo — "Website tip of the day"
  • Offers aur discounts announce karo
  • Note: Sirf woh log receive karenge jinke phone mein tumhara number saved hai

Step 5 — WhatsApp Status — Free Advertising

WhatsApp Status 24 ghante ke liye rehta hai — aur aapke sab contacts use dekh sakte hain:

  • Client ke testimonials post karo
  • Naye website launches share karo
  • Before/after screenshots daalo
  • Special offers announce karo
  • Rozana kam se kam 1-2 status zaroor daalo

Step 6 — Effective Outreach Message Kaise Likhein

Cold messages likhte waqt yeh rules follow karo:

Achha Message — Yeh Format Use Karo:

Namaste Sir! 🙏

[Business Name] Google Maps pe dekha — bahut achha kaam kar rahe hain!

Ek chiz notice ki — kya aapki professional website hai? Aajkal 97% customers Google pe search karke hi visit karte hain.

✅ ₹7,000 mein professional website
✅ 5-7 din mein LIVE

Interested hain? 😊

Bura Message — Yeh Mat Karo:

  • "Website banwao" — too generic, no context
  • Lambe lambe paragraphs — koi nahi padhta
  • Seedha price batana — pehle value batao
  • Ek saath bahut saare links — spam lagta hai

Follow-Up Strategy

  • Day 1: Initial message bhejo
  • Day 3: Ek gentle follow-up — "Kya dekha aapne?"
  • Day 7: Value add karo — ek tip ya case study share karo
  • Day 14: Last follow-up — "Is hafte special offer hai"
Remember: WhatsApp leads aate hain — par convert hote hain website pe. Jab koi WhatsApp pe interested hota hai, usse website ka link bhejo — wahan woh properly sab kuch dekh sake. WhatsApp + Website = Maximum Conversion!
