Instagram Se Business Kaise Badhao — Step by Step Guide 2026

Tarun Singh — TK Web Solutions March 2026 8 min padhne mein Bharatpur, Rajasthan

Aaj 2026 mein Instagram sirf photos share karne ki jagah nahi raha — yeh ek powerful business tool ban gaya hai. Bharatpur ke coaching centers, local shops, aur small businesses ke liye Instagram ek free marketing platform hai jahan rozana lakho potential customers active rehte hain.

Is guide mein hum step by step sikhenge ki Instagram se apna business kaise badhao — bilkul beginner ke liye, Hindi mein.

Important Baat: Instagram aur website — dono saath kaam karte hain. Instagram se traffic aata hai, website pe convert hota hai. Dono hone chahiye!

Step 1 — Business Account Banao

Personal account se business account alag hota hai. Business account mein milta hai:

  • Contact button — Call, Email, WhatsApp directly
  • Insights — kitne log dekh rahe hain, kahan se hain
  • Ads run karne ki facility — ₹100/day se
  • Category — "Local Business", "Education" etc.

Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account → Business select karo. Bilkul free hai!

Step 2 — Bio Perfect Banao

Instagram bio sirf 150 characters ka hota hai — isko wisely use karo:

  • Line 1: Aap kya karte ho — "Website Designer | Bharatpur"
  • Line 2: Unique benefit — "₹5,000 mein Professional Website"
  • Line 3: CTA — "👇 Free Demo ke liye Click Karo"
  • Link: Apni website ka URL daalo — yeh sabse important hai!
Pro Tip: Bio mein location zaroor daalo — "Bharatpur, Rajasthan" — local customers dhundhen ge toh milein ge!

Step 3 — Content Strategy — Kya Post Karein?

Rozana kuch na kuch post karna zaroori hai. Yeh 5 types of content karo:

1. Educational Posts (40%)

"Website ke 5 fayde", "SEO kya hota hai", "Mobile friendly website kyun zaruri hai" — yeh type ke posts log share karte hain aur aapko expert samajhte hain.

2. Before/After Posts (20%)

Client ka kaam dikhao — pehle kaisi website thi, baad mein kaisi bani. Results dikhao — kitne inquiries aaye, Google pe rank kaise aaya.

3. Offer/Promotional Posts (20%)

Apni services, pricing, special offers — but zyada promotional mat karo. 1 post mein 5 educational posts ka ratio rakho.

4. Behind the Scenes (10%)

Apna kaam dikhao — website banate waqt, client meeting, coding — log real content se connect karte hain.

5. Client Reviews (10%)

Happy clients ke testimonials post karo — yeh sabse powerful content hai. Social proof se naye clients aate hain.

Step 4 — Reels Banao — Maximum Reach

Instagram Reels ka algorithm non-followers tak bhi reach karta hai — yeh free viral marketing hai!

  • 30-60 second ke short reels banao
  • Trending audio use karo — reach 3x badhti hai
  • Subtitles daalo — 85% log bina sound ke dekhte hain
  • Hook pehle 3 second mein daalo — "Yeh galti mat karo!"
  • Haar hafte kam se kam 3 reels post karo

Step 5 — Hashtags Sahi Use Karo

Hashtags se aapka content naye logo tak pahunchta hai. Mix use karo:

  • Local hashtags: #bharatpur #bharatpurbusiness #bharatpurcoaching
  • Niche hashtags: #webdesigner #websitedesign #digitalmarketing
  • Hindi hashtags: #websitebanaobharatpur #localbusiness
  • 5-15 hashtags per post — zyada nahi

Step 6 — Engagement Badhao

Sirf post karna kaafi nahi — engagement zaroori hai:

  • Har comment ka reply do — within 1 hour
  • DMs ka turat jawab do — lead miss mat karo
  • Competitors ke followers pe meaningful comments karo
  • Local business accounts ko follow karo, engage karo
  • Stories rozana post karo — polls, questions daalo

Best Posting Times — Bharatpur ke liye

  • Subah 7-9 AM — log uthke phone dekhte hain
  • Dopahar 1-2 PM — lunch break time
  • Shaam 7-9 PM — prime time — maximum engagement
Sabse Badi Baat: Instagram pe link in bio honi chahiye — woh link aapki website honi chahiye. Instagram se interested log website pe aate hain, wahan pe inquiries karte hain. Bina website ke Instagram ka poora fayda nahi!
